Daily Scripture

“Trust in the Lord with all your heart; do not rely on your own understanding.” — Proverbs 3:5

Proverbs 3:5–6 is God’s quiet reminder that you don’t have to hold every piece of life together by yourself. “Trust in the Lord with all your heart” means letting Him carry the places in you that feel stretched, exhausted, or unsure, knowing He understands the weight you’re under even when no one else sees it. “Lean not on your own understanding” is His way of softly telling you that you don’t need to figure everything out — your perspective is shaped by the battles you’ve survived, but His is shaped by the future He’s preparing for you. And when you acknowledge Him in every part of your life, even the small everyday moments, you allow Him to guide you with a tenderness that never forces and never fails. His promise is simple and beautiful: He will direct your path, gently, intentionally, and with a love that refuses to let your sacrifices go unnoticed or your future go unprotected.
